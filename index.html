Frequently Asked Questions

How long do the diffusers last?
Our 200ml reed diffusers typically last between 3 to 4 months depending on room conditions and reeds used.
Are the ingredients truly organic?
Yes, we only use certified organic botanicals sourced directly from sustainable partner farms globally.
Do you offer international shipping?
We currently ship to over 20 countries. Shipping costs and times vary by location.
How often should I flip the reeds?
We recommend flipping them every 1-2 weeks to refresh the scent throw.
Can the bottles be reused?
Absolutely! Our high-quality glass bottles make excellent bud vases once the oil is finished.
